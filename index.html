Placement Procedure

PHASE I: Engagement

• Invitation Sent: Companies receive the invitation and job annexure via email/post.
• Annexure Submission: Companies return the filled annexure with job details.
• Student Access: Job profiles are uploaded to the Placement Online portal.
• PPT Scheduling: Dates for Pre-Placement Talks (PPTs) are finalized.
• Campus Visit: Companies conduct PPTs and may also hold internship selections.



PHASE II: Shortlisting

• Slot Allocation: TPO assigns dates based on student rankings.
• Ranking Criteria:
    - Job profile & growth
    - Compensation
    - Past recruitment record
    - Student feedback
    - Student Registration: Interested students register online.
    - Resume Sharing: Resumes are shared with companies (if required).
    - Shortlist Finalization: Companies send shortlisted candidates to TPO.



PHASE III: Selection

• Campus Interviews: Companies conduct tests, GDs, and interviews.
• Remote Option: Video conferencing is available if needed.
• Final Results: Companies submit the list of selected candidates.
• Placement Policy: Further interview eligibility depends on institute rules.
